Viola – Y.O.L.O

  • Music
Oct 16, 2014
0

Viola Karuri of “Nakupenda” and “Milele” featuring Collo hits has done it again with a new track “Y.O.L.O”.

“Y.O.L.O”, an acronym for “You Only Live Once” is a song advising on making the most of the present. “Life is hard and problems are in every corner”, you only live once so enjoy what you have and make the most of the present. With a chakachaka beat, the Rhythm in “Y.O.L.O” has an African feel good rhythm and strongly encourages living in a happy zone. The combination of a cool vibe, playful informative lyrics and sweet vocals bring out a great song.

Based in Nairobi, Kenya, the solo act that is Viola Karuri is a singing sensation taking the airwaves of both Kenya and in essence all of Africa with her enticing self and captivating lyrics. Viola Karuri’s musical journey has her forging her own unique sound and her memorable look have her fan base that is both loyal and ever growing.

Look out for this fresh, fine sound that is bound to rock your senses and get you hooked to her talent.

Download and Enjoy!
